About Amusement Park

Amuesment Park one of Pakistan's premier Amuesment Park. It is located just 5 minutes from Korangi 3 Town and 30 Minutes from Karachi International Airport (subject to traffic) on 42 beautifully landscaped acres, with huge lush green coconut trees,garden,comfortable and covered food court with dining tables & chairs. More than 25 Exciting water Slides & 12 Swimming Pools. The Quality of water in our Park is the best ,continuously treated and filtered through the most advance filtration System imported from Spain, keeping water sparkling blue, Sunway Lagoon can be compared with any of the international standard Park all over the world.
